Mission

Data. Models. Assumptions. You decide.

The Math Doesn't Lie is a data-driven publication for people who want to see the assumptions behind big decisions.

We look at water, finance, retirement, technology, infrastructure, and policy through the lens of numbers, incentives, and second-order effects.

The goal is not to sound certain. The goal is to make the assumptions visible, testable, and useful.

Articles explain the story. Tools let readers test the math themselves.
